I’m a graphic designer and artist born in the Dominican Republic and raised in South Florida. My work is driven by a passion for print and video design, with a strong emphasis on typography, color, and thoughtful visual storytelling. My journey into design began with poster design, where I developed a deep appreciation for the full design process—from the early stages of research and ideation to refining concepts and bringing a project to its final form. For me, design is not just about creating something visually appealing, but about crafting work that communicates, connects, and leaves a lasting impression. I’m constantly inspired by other art forms such as music, film, and painting, which influences the way I approach narrative in my work. I believe inspiration can come from the smallest details—whether it’s a lyric, a scene from a movie, or a texture in a painting. As a designer, I’m always looking for ways to grow creatively through new ideas, mediums, and collaborations. My goal is to create work that feels intentional, visually engaging, and meaningful to the audience it reaches.
